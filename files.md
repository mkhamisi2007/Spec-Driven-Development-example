| File | For Whom? | Why |
|---|---|---|
| **`spec.md`** | Customer | Only describes "what" is being built, with no technical details — the customer can read and approve it easily |
| **`plan.md`** + `research.md` | Tech Team / Tech Lead | Covers technology and architecture — usually unnecessary and confusing for a non-technical customer |
| **`tasks.md`** | Development Team | Day-to-day executable checklist |
| **Summary of `/speckit.analyze` results** | Everyone (Customer + Team) | Shows everything is consistent and nothing is missing — good as a "final sign-off" shared before implementation |
